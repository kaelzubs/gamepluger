About Us

gameshopa is a free online store run by a bunch of geeks who partake in way too much online window shopping. Our philosophy is simple: we only want to display the coolest stuff you can find and buy on the web. We are not interested in spamming any boring or lame products at you – when you visit gameshopa you’ll only see interesting, innovative, and often times very odd products. Many items listed on this site do return a small commission for product referral, however this is not the sole motivation for featuring an item. Our staff regularly lists products that we receive absolutely no compensation for. We do not carry any of the products listed on the site, nor do we directly sell anything.
